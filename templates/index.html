The 3-Day Curriculum

Three Days. Total Transformation.

The Money Detox

Friday, October 17 • 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Interactive exercise where you write down every money belief holding you back — then we literally shred them on stage. Guided nervous system reset to start from a clean slate.

The Financial Autopsy

Laptops open. We audit your accounts LIVE. Income, expenses, debt, subscriptions — everything gets laid bare. You'll use our custom Money Map worksheet to see exactly where every dollar goes.

The Values Alignment Workshop

Discover your Money Values through our proprietary assessment. Learn why you overspend in some areas and feel guilty in others. Redesign your spending to match what actually makes you happy.

3:15 PM – 5:00 PM

Automation Station

Hands-on setup session. You'll leave with automated transfers to savings, investments, and bills. Bank-specific walkthroughs for Chase, BofA, Wells Fargo, Capital One, and credit unions. Your money will run itself.

5:15 PM – 6:00 PM

🍴 Poolside Happy Hour + Money Story Open Mic

Cocktails by the Fontainebleau pool. Optional open mic where attendees share their money stories. This is where the real breakthroughs happen — when you realize everyone's been faking it.

The Wealth Blueprint

Saturday, October 18 • 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM

9:00 AM – 10:30 AM

Debt Destruction Masterclass

The math AND the emotions of debt payoff. Avalanche vs. snowball methods. Live demonstration: calling a credit card company to negotiate a lower rate (Mr. Easy Money does it on speakerphone). You'll get the exact scripts.

10:45 AM – 12:30 PM

Investing for Humans (Not Finance Bros)

Index funds, ETFs, Roth IRAs, 401(k)s — explained like you're talking to a friend. We'll open a brokerage account together (for those who don't have one) and make your first investment LIVE in the room.

1:30 PM – 3:00 PM

The Salary Negotiation Lab

Role-play negotiation scenarios with a partner. Word-for-word scripts for asking for a raise, negotiating a new offer, and freelance rate-setting. Guest speaker: a Fortune 500 hiring manager reveals what actually works.

3:15 PM – 5:00 PM

Side Hustle Sprint

Identify your highest-value side income opportunity using the Three Ts framework (Time, Skills, Target). By 5 PM, you'll have a name, a price, and your first potential client identified. No vague "follow your passion" advice.

6:30 PM – 8:00 PM

🎉 The Money Glow-Up Gala Dinner

Private dinner at the Fontainebleau's Stripsteak by Michael Mina. Dress code: look like the money you're about to make. Networking, celebration, and a surprise keynote from a self-made millionaire who started with nothing.

3

The Glow-Up

Sunday, October 19 • 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM

9:00 AM – 10:30 AM

The 90-Day Money Action Plan

Build your personalized 90-day roadmap. Week by week, action by action. This isn't a vision board — it's a project plan with deadlines, milestones, and accountability checkpoints built in.

10:45 AM – 12:00 PM

Couples & Money Workshop

How to have productive money conversations with your partner (or anyone you share finances with). Communication frameworks, joint vs. separate accounts strategies, and the Money Date ritual you'll do weekly.

Tax optimization, real estate basics, HSA hacking, backdoor Roth conversions, I-bonds, and credit score maximization. A panel of CFPs, tax professionals, and real estate investors answer YOUR questions live.

Accountability Pod Setup

Get matched with 4-5 people at your level. Exchange contacts, set your first group Money Date, and join your private pod in the Mr. Easy Money app. These pods have a 73% higher success rate than going solo.

A powerful group commitment ceremony. You'll stand up, state your 90-day financial goal to the room, and sign the Glow-Up Pledge. Then we celebrate. Confetti. Music. The works. You leave a different person than you arrived.
